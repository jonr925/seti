The Personal SETI Application allows you to view interstellar radio signals, look for signs of intelligent life.

In order to report your findings to the SETI Institute and your friends and family, please log in via Facebook.

[Log in via Facebook]

[No log in, Read Only]


-----------

Move left and right through the plot to see signals received at different wavelengths.

The brightness of the dot represents the strength of the signal received at a particular moment in time, at a particular wavelength, from this source.

If you see any patterns that are not random, touch and hold on the pattern to report it.
